What is a root canal?

A “root canal” is short for root canal treatment. Which is the removal of pulp tissue from the inside of the tooth and the sealing of that space with a biocompatible material.

The Process

  1. The area is anesthetized or “numbed”.
  2. The tooth is isolated with a rubber dam.
  3. Inside of the roots are cleaned, shaped, and irrigated removing all pulp tissue and bacteria.
  4. Following the disinfection process, the space is filled with biocompatible material.
