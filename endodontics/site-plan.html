Overview

Purpose

Have you ever needed a good dentist, but didn’t know where to go to? That is why I propose to make a website for Dr. Thornton’s dental office to provide patients with the needed information before they even schedule an appointment. The website will contain past patient reviews, an overview of the procedures, and information about Dr. Thornton. It will also have the offices address and phone number so patients can easily schedule appointments when they need.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is patients and potential patients of doctor Justin Thornton. Dr. Thornton specializes in root canals typically for adults, so the age range of the audience is adults ages 30 and older. His dental office is located in Logan, Utah, so the website is mainly targeting people in northern Utah and southeast Idaho. The audience will need to find out information about Dr. Thornton to make a decision if they want to go to him and also for contact information.
